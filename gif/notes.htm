Programming notes How many cards can move from column a to column b? If there are no open columns and n free cells, then you can move n+1; If there is one open column, you can first move n+1 cards to the open column and then n+1 more over to the new location. So: for m free columns and n free cells, you can move a line up to (m+1)*(n+1) cards long. This gets checked in fmove.
